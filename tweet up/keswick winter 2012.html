The Keswick Tweet Up 2012 (#northernTweetUp)

Well, it's happened. What a night/morning that was!

On Saturday 1st December 2012 a group of seven intrepid tweeters gathered in The George hotel in the centre of Keswick, most of them strangers in real life, and by the end of the next day strangers no more.

After food and drinks at the aforementioned establishment there was much stumbling and staggering through a succession of excellent pubs which sold real beer without ANY shiny bar stools or stupid pop music.

By the early hours of the morning a bellyfull of beer / cider / wine / whisky had been poured down every throat and most of us headed back to our warm welcoming beds, though for one of us the lure of Keswick's night life kept him out latest.

We reconvened at the Strawberry the next morning, mostly the worst for wear but all very excited about the winter market that was taking place in Keswick centre, the breakfast that our landlord was cooking for us, and the location of the aforementioned tweeter.

Many sausages were eaten, and much chocolate milkshake, along with eggs and bacon and toasted muffin and mushrooms and tea and bucks fizz (though two of our number cried off the alcohol, so theirs was drunk by one other).

Our number complete, and our bellies replete, we parted ways; each to his or her home (with one person taking 6 hours to get back) and sadly not one lefty injured the entire time. We shall have to try harder next time.

A big shout out to everyone who attended, and thanks for sending the pictures below; evidence of the fun we had.
