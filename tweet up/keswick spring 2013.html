The Keswick Tweet Up 2013 (#northernTweetUp)

So, after the great success of the first keswick tweet up, we have organised another one, this time in Spring 2013. What could possibly be more enjoyable than a weekend in the beautiful town of Keswick, all the little lambs gambolling in the fields and you staggering from bar to bar surrounded by other awesome likeminded individuals.

As the plans become finalised, as per, I will add more details to this page (and tweet about it a LOT) so please do check back regularly. Presumably we will be meeting in a pub, then going to another pub, then another, then maybe a few on to the (one and only) nightclub and then finally convening for an end-of-weekend sausage fest at the Strawberry cafe.

In the 24 hours since the date was finalised I have already had 14 confirmed (some of them bringing partners) and 14 maybes so this looks to be a very well attended event. There is no limit on numbers, however it would be really good if you could tweet @rhmgroo or @lakeslandlord if you are interested, so we can make plans to meet at a venue which will accept such multitudes.

For those requiring accommodation please click here for a websearch I did earlier for you. Just change the dates, sort by distance, and you're rocking.

If you want to have some more details, vote for your food preference, or just generally give someone a hard time please tweet @rhmgroo or @lakeslandlord and one of them will probably help. Maybe. Depending on the time of the day.

ps: thanks to foofig for having the awesome idea of arranging a northern tweet up, seeing as it's not right that all the southerners have all the fun!
